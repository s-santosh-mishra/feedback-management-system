About InsightEdu

InsightEdu is a web-based application developed as part of the Software Engineering assignment by three of M.C.A. students Prajakta Das,Pranish Pradhan and Santosh Kumar Mishra of Veer Surendra Sai University of Technology, Burla.

The objective of this project is to design a structured and role-based system that demonstrates real-world application of software engineering concepts such as modularity, usability, and requirement analysis.

This platform focuses on academic feedback management and performance insight generation for administrators, teachers, and students.
